Personal Android (and Android Wear) applications developed by Jomar Tigcal. Most of these have been published on Google Play Store.

GDG Watch Face

GDG Watch Face is an Android Wear Watch Face which features an analog clock and a digital display. You can customize the watch face by showing/hiding the date and by changing the colors of the hands (hour, minute, second) and hour markers.

Help Me

Help Me is an Android and Android Wear emergency button app that allows you to send an SMS to your specified contact person with a click of a button. It won second place in Accenture Technology Talks & Hackathon 2015.

A tool for checking in participants to an event based on a registration sheet. The app also includes a raffle page which can draw names from the list of checked in participants. This was originally developed for major events of GDG Philippines but it has been used in others' event as well. You can send a message if you are interested about this project.
