About Jomar Tigcal

Jomar Tigcal is a self-taught Android developer with over 10 years experience in mobile Android and software development. He has published more than eight personal apps in the Google Play Store since building his first SIM utility app for his Google Nexus S in 2011.

In addition to building apps for startups and personal use, Jomar is also an active Android trainer who has given talks and conducted workshops and codelabs for university classrooms and with the Google Developer Group Philippines.
